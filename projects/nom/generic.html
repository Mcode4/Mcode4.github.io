Summary

Inspired by Uber Eats, this group project involved me and a team of 4 working together to complete a site we agreed to create. The project came as a unique blend of code and stands out from uber eats in a sense of overall style and effort.

This site allows users to order from restaurants on it and create your own restaurants. Also users could re-order from orders already made and favorite item. Accounts come with mock money to better simulate how a site like this would work too. We've also added some restrictions on only ordering from one restaurant at a time and disabling the cart without delivery information do to it being an online delivery site.
